EECS BBQ

While Northwestern students are known for their drive and commendable work ethic, we also like to have fun! Once a year, during our spring quarter, the members of IEEE NU fire up a few grills and host a barbecue for all Northwestern students, faculty, and staff. This event, known as the EECS BBQ, is funded by Northwestern's EECS department and gives everyone a chance to relax and connect with others in the Northwestern community and learn about IEEE.
